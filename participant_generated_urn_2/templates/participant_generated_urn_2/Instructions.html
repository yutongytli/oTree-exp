Instructions

In this experiment you can bet on the result of one coin flip and one ball drawn from an urn. The two bets differ as follows:

A standard coin with "Heads" and "Tails" will be thrown randomly by the computer. "Heads" and "Tails" are equally likely. It is impossible that a coin lands on its edge.

A ball with specific symbol will be drawn from an urn. The contents of the urn are decided as follows:
First, each participant in your group chooses a ball with symbol (either "{{ htmla }}" or "{{ htmlb }}") to put in the urn. The contents of this virtual urn are determined by what symbol the other subjects chose. The only information you have at this stage is the total number of balls in your urn as shown below.
