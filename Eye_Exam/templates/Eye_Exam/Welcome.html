This activity is part of a study in decision-making. You will be grouped with another worker(your counterpart) and you two will be asked to finish three tasks.

In the first task, you will be asked to transcribe a paragraph with a specific content. The data entry is to ensure that you are human and not computers.

In the second task, you will be presented with 36 images of eyes. For each pair of eyes, choose which word best describes what the person in the picture is thinking or feeling.
You will be paid 80 cents for completing this task!

In the third task, you will be asked to participate in a Two-player Communication Game or answer two questions.
You will be paid a certain amount of money according to your decision!

Please be patient to wait for your partner and complete all the tasks, otherwise your HIT will not be accepted and you will not be paid.
